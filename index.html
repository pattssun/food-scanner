Food waste created yearly amounts to hundreds of billions pounds in the US alone and is disposed of in landfills. This poses a problem aggregated not only by the non-biodegradable debris, but the food itself contributes to the emission of green house gasses as a result of rotting. We are addressing this issue by designing a system that reduces waste and furthermore helps users to make informed decisions about their health.

Users can take a picture of your food and upload it to our website. The system will scan and detect the name of your food and offer nutritional information as well as determine if a food is safe to eat or rotten. Our goal strives to both better ourselves while bettering the environment.
